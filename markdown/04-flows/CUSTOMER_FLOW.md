# Customer Flow

## Browse Flow
1. Customer opens homepage.
2. Customer sees featured products.
3. Customer opens product listing.
4. Customer opens product details.

## Cart Flow
1. Customer clicks add to cart.
2. Product is added to local cart.
3. Customer can increase or reduce quantity.
4. Customer can remove product.

## Checkout Flow
1. Customer opens cart.
2. Customer clicks checkout.
3. If not signed in, customer must sign in with Google.
4. Customer confirms order details.
5. App creates pending order.
6. App initializes payment.
7. Customer completes payment.
8. App verifies payment.
9. App marks order as paid.
10. App shows success page and WhatsApp button.

## WhatsApp Flow
1. Customer sees “Message Helima on WhatsApp”.
2. Button opens WhatsApp with pre-filled order message.
3. Message includes order ID, order items, total, customer name, and email.
