# Product Specification — Helima

## One-Line Description
Helima is a Next.js + Firebase ecommerce website for selling imported products, with Google login, admin product management, payment, and WhatsApp follow-up after purchase.

## Target Users
### Customer
A person who wants to browse and buy imported products online.

### Admin
The Helima business owner or staff member who manages products, orders, and payment records.

## MVP Features
### Customer Features
- View homepage
- View product list
- View product details
- Sign in with Google
- Add products to cart
- Remove products from cart
- Update cart quantity
- Checkout
- Pay in-app
- See payment success page
- Click WhatsApp link after payment

### Admin Features
- Admin-only dashboard
- View products
- Create product
- Edit product
- Delete product
- Upload product images
- View orders
- View order details
- Update order status

## Non-MVP Features
Do not build these in the first version:
- Multiple vendors
- Student accounts
- Auctions
- Product reviews
- Coupons
- Loyalty points
- AI recommendations
- Complex delivery tracking
- Wallet system
- Crypto payments

## Success Criteria
The MVP is complete when:
- A customer can browse products.
- A customer can sign in using Google only.
- A customer can add items to cart.
- A customer can checkout and make payment.
- A paid order is saved in Firebase.
- A WhatsApp link appears after payment.
- Admin can manage products.
- Admin can view orders.
